Sunflower Shop

Operating Hours

Sunflower Shop operates from 9 am till 6 pm. 7 days a week.

Ownership

Sunflower Shop was established as a sole proprietorship in Massachusetts, USA in the year of 2001.
In short five years Sunflower established it's self as number one floral shop in Massachusetts.

In the near future Sunflower is looking to partner with floral boutiques in all around New England.

History Name

Sunflower Shop name originated from a flower grown in Russia called sunflower.
The sunflower petals represent the rays of the sun which make many people smile and happy.
